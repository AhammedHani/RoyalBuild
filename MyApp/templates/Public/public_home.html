About Us

Welcome to Royal Associates,

Discover the simplicity of building with Royal Associates, your trusted brick manufacturer. Situated in Elettil Vattoli, our factory produces a range of superior building materials – Bricks, Pots, Sands, Metals, Interlocks, and more.

Your Convenient Building Partner,

We've crafted this online store with one goal in mind – to provide you with an easy and convenient shopping experience. Say goodbye to the frustrations of traditional material procurement. Now, you can order bricks and building essentials from anywhere, at any time, using your phone or computer.

Why Royal Associates?

  • Quality Materials: Our bricks and building supplies meet the highest standards.
  • Variety: From bricks to interlocks, find everything you need in one place.
  • Convenience: Shop effortlessly online, saving time and eliminating hassle.

Revolutionize Building with Royal Associates - Quality, Simplified.
